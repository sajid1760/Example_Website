Character Description:

I was born in a lonely place somewhere in our vast Universe. Some say the planet was in the Andromeda Galaxy, but they say that to make me feel better. I feel I am from a lonely planet in a small galaxy with very few life forms. I cannot prove this fact, but I am on a quest to discover my past.

My biology is rather peculiar. I am compelled to wear a special space suit at all times to avoid being poisoned by the local atmosphere. Everybody looks at me funny always. It is rather annoying and very difficult to get used to.

Even though I am far removed from my home planet, I fear electronic gremlins have connected me to a vast and invisible intergalactic neural network. I am struggling to deal with this new reality.
